Core Valuation Models

Common frameworks used by analysts and CFA charterholders.

Price‑Earnings (P/E)

Compares share price to earnings per share to gauge relative value. Lower P/E may suggest undervaluation.

Price‑to‑Book (P/B)

Measures share price against book value per share. A P/B below 1 can indicate a bargain relative to assets.

Dividend Discount

Estimates intrinsic value by discounting expected dividends back to the present.

Discounted Cash Flow

Values a company based on the present value of projected free cash flows.

EV/EBITDA

Compares enterprise value to earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization.

Residual Income

Assesses value by adding book value to the present value of forecasted excess earnings.
